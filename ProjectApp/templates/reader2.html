How Does It Work?

  • Search the book you want by Name or Author
  • If the book is available you can borrow it by clicking the 'borrow' button
  • You can borrow a book for a period of 14 days
  • Please remember to return the book at the end of the borrow period
  • If the copy you hold isn't ordered by another reader, you can extend the duration of the order by 7 days
  • If the book you were looking for it's not available right now, you can order it
  • If we don't have the book you are looking for, we are very sorry
